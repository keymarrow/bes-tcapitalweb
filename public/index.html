Common questions about financial advice

Everything you need to know about working with a financial planner in Tanzania.

A financial adviser helps you make sense of your money and plan for the future. At Bes-tCapital, we start by understanding your life goals - whether that's retiring early, funding your children's education, or simply having peace of mind about your finances. We then create a comprehensive plan covering investments, pensions, tax efficiency, and protection, all tailored to your situation. Based in Dar es salaam, Tanzania., we focus on giving you clarity and confidence about your financial future.

We believe in transparent, fixed fees rather than percentage-based charges that grow with your wealth. Our fixed-fee approach means you know exactly what you'll pay, and it's often significantly less than the industry standard of 1% per year. We offer a free initial consultation so you can understand exactly what's involved before making any commitment.

Many people successfully manage their own finances, but an adviser can add significant value in several ways: avoiding costly mistakes during market volatility, optimising your tax position through inheritance tax planning, ensuring you're on track for retirement, and providing peace of mind. Research consistently shows that advised clients achieve better outcomes - not just financially, but in terms of confidence and reduced stress about money.

Our advisers hold Chartered Financial Planner status, Fellow of the Personal Finance Society (FPFS), and Trust and Estate Practitioner (TEP) qualifications from the Chartered Insurance Institute and STEP, demonstrating the highest level of professional qualifications and commitment to ongoing development. Our team combines this expertise with our evidence-based approach grounded in 60+ years of academic research.

Getting started is simple. Book a free, no-obligation discovery call with one of our advisers. During this 30-minute conversation, we'll listen to your situation, understand what's on your mind, and explain how we might help. We serve clients across Tanzania and the East Africa at large, just an honest conversation about your financial planning needs.
